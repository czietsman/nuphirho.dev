Fable access is back baby (almost)!

The export controls have been lifted and Anthropic is redeploying the models. Which is good news for the companies that lost access without warning.

But I want to flag something. The post below was about access risk, not about whether this particular shutdown would be permanent. The point stands. A government order came through on a Friday afternoon. Access was cut globally within hours. Anthropic complied.

The switch goes both ways. That is exactly the point.
